Blog

1. How to Control Blood Pressure Naturally

High blood pressure is a silent killer. Reduce salt, manage stress, exercise regularly, and monitor your readings. Small lifestyle changes can reduce dependency on medications.

2. Early Signs of Diabetes You Shouldn’t Ignore

Excessive thirst, frequent urination, fatigue, and unexplained weight loss are early signs. Get tested early — diabetes is manageable with the right care.

3. Understanding Thyroid Disorders

Thyroid problems can lead to fatigue, weight changes, and mood swings. Both hyperthyroidism and hypothyroidism require regular monitoring and medication adjustments for best control.

4. Managing Obesity: It’s Not Just About Diet

Obesity is a complex medical issue. Addressing hormonal imbalances, stress, sleep quality, and underlying conditions is as important as diet and exercise.

5. GERD – More Than Just Heartburn

Gastroesophageal Reflux Disease (GERD) causes acid reflux and chest discomfort. Lifestyle changes like early dinners, reducing spicy food, and elevating head while sleeping help significantly.
